Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment through our website by visiting the home page and using the booking form. Select your preferred location, service, date, and provide your email. Alternatively, you can call our appointment hotline at +91 22 1234 5678.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major insurance plans including CGHS, ECHS, Star Health, Aditya Birla Health Insurance, HDFC ERGO, ICICI Lombard, and many others. Please contact our insurance department at insurance@healthoptimiser.com for specific inquiries about your plan.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

For your first appointment, please bring:

  • Valid photo ID (Aadhar, PAN, Driving License)
  • Insurance card (if applicable)
  • List of current medications
  • Previous medical records and test results
  • Referral letter (if required)
  • Payment method for any co-pays or fees

How do I access my health records?

You can access your health records through our Health Passport feature on our website. This digital health record system allows you to view your medical history, test results, prescriptions, and upcoming appointments. For assistance, please contact our patient support team.

What are your visiting hours?

Our general visiting hours are:

  • Weekdays: 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM
  • Weekends: 11:00 AM to 5:00 PM

Specific departments may have different visiting hours. Please check with the respective department or call our helpline for more information.

How can I get a prescription refill?

You can request a prescription refill through our Online Medication service on our website. Alternatively, you can call our pharmacy at +91 22 9876 1234 or email your request to pharmacy@healthoptimiser.com. Please provide your patient ID, prescription details, and contact information.
